Safety Standards

Our commitment against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

Zero Tolerance Policy

Bluemica maintains a Zero Tolerance Policy toward any content or behavior related to the sexual abuse or exploitation of children (CSAE). We are committed to fostering a safe campus environment and complying with global safety standards to prevent the spread of illegal material.

Prohibited Content & Conduct

Under no circumstances does Bluemica allow:

  • The sharing, posting, or transmission of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).
  • Any form of grooming behavior or predatory interaction.
  • The promotion or facilitation of child exploitation or trafficking.
  • Links to external sites hosting illegal exploitation material.

Protective Measures

We utilize manual moderation and automated reporting tools to identify and remove prohibited content. Any detected illegal material is immediately removed, and the associated account is permanently banned.

Mandatory Reporting

In accordance with the law, Bluemica reports all instances of CSAM/CSAE to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement agencies.

See something? Report it.

If you encounter any content that violates our safety standards, use the in-app reporting tool or contact our safety team immediately.
