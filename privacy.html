Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

1. Institute Affiliation

Access to the Bluemica app is strictly restricted to current members of the institute. To ensure a safe and exclusive environment, all users must register using their official institute email address (@iiserb.ac.in).

Verification is mandatory. Accounts created with personal email addresses (Gmail, Outlook, etc.) will not be allowed.

2. Account Information & Integrity

During registration, you are required to provide accurate and truthful information, including:

  • Full legal name
  • Official Institute Email
  • Gender (for safe Ride-Sharing matches)
  • Course Program / Batch details

Note: Each user is permitted to maintain only one active account. You are responsible for the confidentiality of your credentials.

3. Information Collection & Privacy

We collect your name, email, gender, and course program specifically to verify your identity and foster a trusted, transparent campus community. This data is never sold to third-party advertisers.

Media & Gallery Access

We deeply respect your personal privacy. The Bluemica app will never scan, read, or harvest your local device gallery.

Media permissions are requested strictly and only to allow you to manually select and upload specific photos or videos for:

  • Community Posts
  • Direct Chat Attachments
  • Buy/Sell Marketplace and lost & found Listings
