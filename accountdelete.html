Requesting Bluemica Account Deletion

We value your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from our systems, please follow the instructions below.

To initiate a deletion request, please email us at:
contact@bluemica.online
Important Information:
  • Processing Time: Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 20 days of receiving your request.
  • Data Removed: Deletion includes your profile information, college email identifier, all listings, and associated content.
  • Irreversibility: Once the process is complete, the data cannot be recovered.
